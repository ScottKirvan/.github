# Support

## Before opening an issue

- Search the [existing issues](../../issues) — your question may already be answered.
- Check the project's documentation (linked in the repo README).

## Getting help

| Channel | Best for |
|---|---|
| [GitHub Issues](../../issues/new/choose) | Bug reports, feature requests, documentation problems |
| [Discord](https://discord.gg/TN6XJSNK5Y) | General questions, discussion, quick answers |

## Reporting a bug

Use the **Bug Report** issue template and include:
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Steps to reproduce
- Your environment (OS, app/plugin version)

The more detail you provide, the faster it gets resolved.

## Feature requests

Use the **Feature Request** issue template. Explain the problem you're trying to solve, not just the solution — it helps with scoping and prioritization.

## Response times

These are personal projects maintained in spare time. I try to respond within a few days but can't guarantee it. Reproducible bug reports with good detail get prioritized.
